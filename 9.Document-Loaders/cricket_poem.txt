On a green field where the willow sings,
The crack of leather, the roar of wings.
A bowler runs with steady grace,
A batsman greets the coming pace.

The sun hangs low, the shadows grow,
The crowd is hushed in evening glow.
A spin, a seam, a swinging arc,
A match lit bright within the dark.

The stumps stand proud, the fielders wait,
Each ball can shift a fragile fate.
A captain plans, a partnership builds,
A game of patience, passion, skills.

At every turn, a story told,
Of courage strong and spirits bold.
For love of sport and battle’s art,
Cricket binds us heart to heart.
